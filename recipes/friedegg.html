Teyvat Fried Egg

Ingredients:

  • 1 Egg
  • Butter or Oil
  • Salt and Pepper to taste
  • Optional: Chives for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Heat a pan over medium heat and add a small amount of butter or oil.
  2. Crack the egg into the pan and cook until the whites are set but the yolk is still runny, about 2-3 minutes.
  3. Place the fried egg on top of a dish.
  4. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  5. Serve immediately and enjoy a beautiful Teyvat Fried Egg!
